Get those Class Lists!

D2L 30 seconds per class

  1. Log in to D2L.

  2. Click on one of your classes that you're in. Once there, click the tab/page called "Classlist" which is usually on the main navigation.

  3. Click the button at the top that says, "Email Classlist". Don't worry, this won't email them!

  4. Now copy the entire classlist, (First & Last Names, and E-mails if available) and paste it into an excel spreadsheet.

    If the list is weirdly formatted when you try to paste it, try pasting it in excel this way: "Right click" and then select, "Paste Special". Then select as "Text".

  5. Make the title of the tab (bottom of the sheet) the name of the course, ex: ECON 200.

  6. Please repeat this process for each of your D2L classes by making a new tab for each class. Save the Excel sheet and include it in the email you send back to us. Don't forget to attach your syllabi as well!

Blackboard 1 min per class

  1. Log in to Blackboard.

  2. Click on one of your classes that you're in. Once there, click "Tools" on the left side menu.

  3. Scroll about half way down the page on the right column and click "Roster".

  4. Change the second search filter from "Contains" to "Not blank". Then press "Go".

  5. Before copying anything, scroll down to the bottom right of the page and click "Show All".

  6. Now copy the entire classlist, (First & Last Names, and E-mails if available) and paste it into an excel spreadsheet.

    If the list is weirdly formatted when you try to paste it, try pasting it in excel this way: "Right click" and then select, "Paste Special". Then select as "Text".

  7. Make the title of the tab (bottom of the sheet) the name of the course, ex: ECON 200.

  8. Please repeat this process for each of your Blackboard classes by making a new tab for each class. Save the Excel sheet and include it in the email you send back to us. Don't forget to attach your syllabi as well!

Other 1 min per class

  1. Log in to the website where your course is managed by your instructor, such as D2L, Moodle, or Blackboard.

  2. Click on one of your classes that you're in. Once there, click on a tab/page that is similar to "Tools", "Classlist", "Roster", or "Email".

  3. Now copy the entire classlist, (First & Last Names, and E-mails if available) and paste it into an excel spreadsheet.

    If the list is weirdly formatted when you try to paste it, try pasting it in excel this way: "Right click" and then select, "Paste Special". Then select as "Text".

  4. Make the title of the tab (bottom of the sheet) the name of the course, ex: ECON 200.

  5. Please repeat this process for each of your classes by making a new tab for each class. Save the Excel sheet and include it in the email you send back to us. Don't forget to attach your syllabi as well!
